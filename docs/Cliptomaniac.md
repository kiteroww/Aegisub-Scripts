# Cliptomaniac 0.2.16

Cliptomaniac is a clip toolbox for measuring, transforming, reshaping, fitting, projecting, and converting ASS clips.

Menu root: `Cliptomaniac`

Namespace: `kite.Cliptomaniac`

## Main areas

- Clip measurement and transform helpers.
- Clip scaling, margin expansion, toggling, copying, and diagnostics.
- Text-to-clip, clip-to-shape, shape-to-clip, and boolean clip workflows.
- Perspective and projected-quad operations.
- Frame-by-frame manual move helpers.

## Dedicated entries

- `Cliptomaniac`
- `Cliptomaniac/Help`
- `: Kite Hotkeys :/Cliptomaniac/...`

The hotkey branch exposes individual Cliptomaniac operations for direct assignment.

## Configuration

Settings persist through ConfigHandler in `kite-cliptomaniac.json`.
